What this policy covers

This policy explains what personal data GuardLet collects, why we collect it, how long we keep it, who we share it with, and what rights you have over it. It applies to:

  • Visitors to guardlet.co.uk, including anyone who joins the waitlist
  • Landlords who create a GuardLet account once the product launches
  • Tenant data that a landlord uploads or enters into GuardLet on their tenant's behalf

What data we collect

From the waitlist and this website

  • Name and email address (submitted via our waitlist form)
  • Number of properties you manage, if provided
  • Basic technical data such as browser type and general location, collected automatically by our hosting provider for security and performance purposes

From your GuardLet account (once launched)

  • Your name, email address and account login details
  • Property addresses and details you add to your account
  • Safety certificates and compliance documents you upload (e.g. Gas Safety Certificates, EICRs, EPCs)
  • Tenancy information you enter, including tenant names, contact details and deposit amounts
  • Right to Rent check records, where you use this feature. This may include details relating to a tenant's immigration status, which UK GDPR treats as requiring extra care
  • Payment and billing information, processed directly by Stripe — GuardLet does not store your card details

Your role and ours, when it comes to tenant data

When you, as a landlord, enter information about your tenants into GuardLet, you are the data controller for that tenant data — you decide what's collected and why. GuardLet acts as a data processor, storing and processing that data only to provide the service to you. You're responsible for having a lawful basis to hold and share your tenant's information, including under any tenancy agreement or applicable law.
