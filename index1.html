What do we really offer

We're not selling a product. We're selling your time back.

A certificate tracker is just software. What it actually does is take the four or five hours a month you'd spend checking dates, chasing paperwork and reading GOV.UK updates, and turns it into a five-minute task you do once and forget about.

⏱️
What it costs you now
Most self-managing landlords spend an evening a month cross-checking certificate dates, digging through email for the right PDF, and reading up on whatever's changed in the Renters' Rights Act. Across a year, that's a working week spent on admin that produces nothing but peace of mind.
📤
What you actually do in GuardLet
You upload a certificate once. GuardLet reads the expiry date off it, files it against the right property, and sets the reminders. That's the entire interaction. No data entry, no spreadsheet columns, no remembering to check back later.
✅
What happens after that
GuardLet watches the dates for you. It emails you at 60, 30 and 7 days before anything lapses, flags new regulations that affect your properties, and keeps a compliance score so you can see your risk at a glance, without opening a single document.
