The stakes

The fines are real.
Get ahead of them.

Most landlords miss deadlines because nothing prompts them in time.

£30,000
Maximum fine for a missing EICR
Electrical safety certificates expire every 5 years. Most landlords find out theirs has lapsed when a council notice arrives.
30 days
To protect a deposit — or risk a penalty
Failing to register a deposit within 30 days invalidates your possession rights and exposes you to compensation claims of up to 3× the deposit amount.
15+
New requirements from the Renters' Rights Act 2025
The Renters' Rights Act abolished Section 21. Rent increases now require a Section 13 notice. Pet requests need a written response within 28 days. That's three new obligations from one piece of legislation.
